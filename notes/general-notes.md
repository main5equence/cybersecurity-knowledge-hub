# General Notes

## Overview

This section contains general notes, observations, and summaries from my cybersecurity learning journey.

It includes key insights, simplified explanations, and reflections that support a better understanding of cybersecurity concepts.

---

## Key Observations

- Cybersecurity is not only about technology but also about human behavior  
- Many attacks exploit simple mistakes rather than complex vulnerabilities  
- Security is often a balance between protection and usability  

---

## Learning Insights

- Understanding fundamentals (e.g., CIA Triad) helps in analyzing advanced topics  
- Real-world examples make concepts easier to understand  
- Threat awareness is as important as technical knowledge  

---

## Reflections

- Security systems should be designed with users in mind  
- Overly complex security can lead to unsafe behavior  
- Continuous learning is essential due to evolving threats
